We invest in smart people who have a clear vision to solve difficult problems.

We believe our purpose as capital providers is to earn an attractive return by funding positive transformation in companies and people.

Our investment philosophy

It's about more than tech.

Flowstate Capital focuses on high potential, purpose driven companies in the early stages of their growth cycle. Start ups? Yes. Tech start ups? Not always.

We invest in companies that delight and inspire not just their customers but also our team. While we appreciate the importance of technology in all forms, we are equally as impressed by physical products and brick and mortar operations.

It's about more than money.

We provide what small businesses need to achieve real growth. Often, this involves funding money but it’s also about expert mentorship, technical knowledge, and the creation of synergies.

Ultimately, we believe in community. We are building a network of people, businesses and customers that support each other, and grow together. We operate in markets categorised by intimacy rather than massive size and we believe in utilizing this to improve results.

It's about radical growth.

We recognise that radical business growth is about more than revenue and profit - it’s about building teams, honing vision, and executing smart rather than hard. We believe that many small businesses forget about these things in the rush for users, and funding.

What we offer.

Funding

We provide early-stage seed capital to take companies from the garage to the demo floor. Funds can be applied to build a prototype, buy equipment, or hire a team.

Support

Often young companies lack the expertise they need to move their companies forward. We provide support with legal and financial services, branding and digital marketing, as well as technology and infrastructure.

Network

An investment from us brings with it a powerful network. We are connected to hundreds of skilled entrepreneurs, the Cape Town start-up community, and seasoned business executives.
